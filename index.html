Web and SEO

Search engine optimization (SEO) helps small and medium businesses show up when people search for what you offer. We focus on practical improvements that make your site easier for search engines to understand and for visitors to use — so you earn relevant traffic without depending only on paid ads.

What we cover

  • Technical SEO: Site structure, crawlability, page speed, mobile usability, HTTPS, and fixes for indexing issues so search engines can find and rank your pages.
  • On-page SEO: Clear titles and descriptions, sensible headings, internal linking, and content aligned with how your customers search.
  • SEO optimization: Iterative improvements after launch — refining content and metadata, fixing issues surfaced in Search Console, and tuning pages toward your target queries.
  • Robots and sitemap: A proper robots.txt (crawl hints for bots) and an XML sitemap listing important URLs so search engines can discover and prioritize your site structure.
  • Local and regional visibility: Where it fits your business, we align your site and listings so you appear for location-based queries (e.g. services in your area).
  • Measurement: Baseline checks and ongoing monitoring so you can see what changes and what to refine next.

Why it matters for SMEs

Many of the sites we build and support for SMEs compete in crowded markets. Solid SEO improves discoverability, supports credibility, and compounds over time — alongside a fast, accessible site and clear messaging. If you already work with us on web projects, SEO can be integrated into delivery; we can also review and improve existing sites.
